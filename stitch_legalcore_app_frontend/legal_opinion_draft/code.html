Survey No. 2/A Ref: VOLT-2024-089

Legal Opinion on Title

calendar_today Drafted 24 May 2024

1. Description of Property

The property under investigation comprises a parcel of land measuring approximately 4.5 acres situated in the industrial hub of Sector 12, characterized by its clear boundaries and accessibility to the primary highway artery. The plot is currently zoned for commercial development.

2. Chronology of Title

Title history spans four decades, originating from the initial government allotment in 1984. Subsequent transfers via registered sale deeds (1992, 2005) have been verified against the Registrar's digital records to ensure continuity and legitimacy of the chain of custody.

3. Present Ownership

The current owner of record is Kinetic Volt Holdings Corp. Verification of the recent mutation records indicates that all property taxes are current, and the owner maintains full possessory rights as of the date of this draft.

4. Encumbrances

A comprehensive search conducted at the Sub-Registrar's Office for the past 30 years reveals no outstanding mortgages, liens, or legal attachments. The certificate of non-encumbrance is pending final verification for the current fiscal quarter.

5. Observations

Note: Minor discrepancy found in boundary wall alignment on the Eastern flank. Recommended on-site survey to reconcile physical dimensions with the cadastral map before final execution of the deed.

6. Opinion

Based on the provided documentation and independent verification, the title is considered Marketable and Free from reasonable doubt, subject to the resolution of the boundary alignment mentioned in section 5.
